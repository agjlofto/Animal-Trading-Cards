Black Panther

A black panther is the melanistic color variant of any Panthera species.

  • Scientific Name: Panthera onca
  • Location: Americas
  • Physical Characteristics: Melanistic
  • Allele: Dominant

Black jaguars may produce either black or spotted cubs, but a pair of spotted jaguars can only produce spotted cubs. Recently, preliminary studies also suggest that melanism might be linked to beneficial mutations in the immune system.
